Delete your Vyeya account & data

Vyeya has no servers and no sign-up: your "account" is a cryptographic key that exists only on your phone. That means deletion is done on your phone, and it is immediate.

In the app (recommended)

  1. Open Vyeya → Profile tab.
  2. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This permanently: erases your account key and phone number from the phone's secure keystore, wipes all local data, and publishes deletion notices so other phones drop your profile and listings from the network.

If you no longer have the app

Uninstalling the app removes your account key and phone number from your phone (they exist nowhere else). Your public profile and listings then expire from the network automatically (listings within 7 days, profiles within 365 days). If you want them hidden network-wide sooner, email us the name and village on the profile at pardhu.parimi@gmail.com and we will hide them using the app's moderation mechanism.

What deletion covers — honestly

DataDeleted?
Account key, phone number, all data on your phoneYes — immediately and permanently
Your profile & listings on other users' phonesYes — deletion notices propagate; also auto-expire
Ratings/statements other users wrote about youNo — they are other people's signed statements; but once your key is destroyed nothing links them to you
Copies on public Nostr relaysReplaced/expired per those operators' policies; email us to request network-wide hiding

Deleting your account is irreversible. If you may return, create a backup code first (Profile → Back up account) — it is the only way to recover the same identity and reputation.
