Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 · Vyeya (Android app, package com.vyeya.app) · Contact: vyeya92@gmail.com

Vyeya is a free marketplace app that connects Indian farmers and buyers directly. It is serverless: the developer operates no backend, and the app never sends any data to the developer. This policy explains exactly what the app does with your data — including the parts that are public by design.

Who we are

Vyeya is published by an individual developer (contact above). Because there is no server, the developer cannot see, collect, store, or sell any of your data. Everything below happens between your phone, other users' phones, and the public infrastructure named explicitly in this policy.

What is PUBLIC by design

Your profile (name, village, state, crops), your listings, and the ratings you give are signed public records. That is the product: buyers must be able to find sellers. These records travel:

  • phone-to-phone over Bluetooth/Wi-Fi to nearby Vyeya users, and
  • over the internet via public Nostr relays — third-party servers we do not operate: relay.damus.io, nos.lol, relay.primal.net, nostr.mom, relay.nostr.bg, offchain.pub.

Anyone on the internet can read public records from those relays, and relay operators may retain copies indefinitely — the app's expiry rules bind phones, not relays. Your profile name is also visible to nearby phones while the Bluetooth radio is on (it is the device's advertising name).

What is PRIVATE by design

Your phone number never leaves your phone unencrypted. It is shared in exactly two moments, both initiated by you: when you send a purchase request (sealed to that farmer), and when you respond to a request (sealed to that buyer). Sealing is end-to-end encryption (X25519 + XChaCha20-Poly1305): relays and relaying phones carry ciphertext they cannot read.

Honest limitation: even for sealed messages, the envelope is public — i.e. the fact that your key sent a request to another key, and when. Treat the who-contacted-whom graph as visible.

Voice input

The microphone is used only while you use the mic button on the Sell screen. Your phone's own speech recogniser converts speech to text; Vyeya receives only the text, uses it to fill the form, and never stores or transmits audio. Depending on your device and language pack, Android's recogniser may send audio to Google's servers for transcription — that step is governed by your device's Google settings, not by Vyeya.

Permissions

  • Bluetooth / Nearby devices — finding and syncing with nearby phones.
  • Location — requested only on Android 12 and older, because those Android versions require it for Bluetooth scanning. Vyeya contains no GPS code and never reads, stores, or shares your position. On Android 13+ the app declares its Bluetooth scanning as neverForLocation and requests no location permission at all.
  • Microphone — voice input only, requested when you first tap the mic.
  • Internet — the public-relay bridge above, and fetching official mandi prices from api.data.gov.in (the request includes your state name and, inherently, your IP address; nothing else).

Deleting your data

  • Delete a listing any time (long-press it) — the deletion propagates through the network like any update.
  • Profile → Delete account permanently erases your account key, phone number, and all local data, and publishes deletion notices so other phones drop your profile and listings. See Delete your account.
  • Honest limits of deletion on a peer-to-peer network: ratings written by other people are their signed statements and cannot be deleted — but once your key is destroyed, nothing links them to you. Copies of public records already on public relays may persist there under those operators' policies.
  • Records also expire automatically on every phone: listings after 7 days, requests after 14, profiles and ratings after 365.

What we DON'T do

No ads. No analytics. No tracking SDKs. No payments (the app never handles money). No developer telemetry of any kind — the app has no way to phone home because there is no home to phone.

Children

Vyeya is intended for adult farmers and buyers (18+) and is not directed at children.
